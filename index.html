About


Dr. Nakeema Stefflbauer spent nine years and earned three degrees immersed in Modern Middle East History, including North Africa (migration & integration) and the Near East (informal trade & labor flows). In 1999, she switched from research to leading eLearning software projects for the NYSE, NYMEX, and for New York University.


After 16 years of leading e-Commerce, ERP, and mobile software projects in the US, Canada, and Europe, Dr. Stefflbauer founded FrauenLoop to pro-actively address inclusion and empowerment of under-represented women in technology-driven industries.
