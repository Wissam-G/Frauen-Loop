ABOUT

Dr. Nakeema Stefflbauer spent nine years and earned three degrees immersed in Modern Middle East History, including North Africa (migration & integration) and the Near East (informal trade & labor flows). In 1999, she switched from research to leading eLearning software projects for the NYSE, NYMEX, and for New York University School of Continuing & Professional Studies.

After 15 years of launching e-Commerce, ERP, and mobile software projects in the US, Canada, and Europe, Dr. Stefflbauer created the initial program for the ReDI School of Digital Integration and for the Refugees on Rails Berlin chapter. Seeing an unmet need, she founded FrauenLoop as a non-profit organization to include, integrate and empower under-represented women in technology-driven industries.

ORGANIZATION DETAILS

FrauenLoop is a Berlin-based non-profit organization supported by software industry professionals. We offer free training and mentorship to motivated EU resident and newcomer women who wish to change to tech industry careers. Over three 3-month cycles, we cover the basics of full-stack web development using Ruby on Rails, interactive design using JavaScript, and database organization and management using Rails and SQL. Our students are mentored by professional developers at Andela, a ground-breaking software company supported by the Zuckerberg-Chan Foundation.
