WHY WOMEN

The typical college graduate will change jobs many times.
Sometimes it's a choice - but for many women it isn't. MORE



Women’s economic independence derives from access to the job market.
Access can be dramatically improved by technology skill development.MORE



Without technology skills - or digital literacy - women are reduced to simply consuming technology
…leaving us with products and services that don’t reflect the women who use them.
